Privacy Notice

When you use Tandem Collabo, the app collects the following information:

  • Original text: The text you provide from your conversation partner.
  • Corrected text: Your corrected version of the original text.
  • Notes: Any additional notes you choose to enter along with your correction.
  • Your IP address: This is used to help diagnose server problems and to prevent abuse.

The information that collect is used to provide you with the service of this app, improve the app, and understand how users interact with the app. Information will not be shared, sold, rented, or traded with third parties for their commercial purposes.

If you wish to have your information removed from our database or have any concerns about the data we hold, please email tandem@omg.lol.

By using Tandem Collabo, you agree to the collection and use of the abovementioned information.

Using Tandem Collabo for illegal purposes, or in any manner that intends to harm, harass, or intimidate another person, is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

  1. Posting or sharing content that is illegal, harmful, threatening, abusive, harassing, defamatory, vulgar, obscene, invasive of another's privacy, hateful, or racially, ethnically, or otherwise objectionable.
  2. Engaging in behavior that compromises the safety or security of others.
  3. Impersonating any person or entity, or falsely stating or otherwise misrepresenting your affiliation with a person or entity.
  4. Utilizing the platform to send unsolicited advertising or promotions, spam, or any other form of solicitation.

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe is unsuitable, or if something makes you feel unsafe or uncomfortable, we urge you to report it immediately.

Please email tandem@omg.lol with the following details:
